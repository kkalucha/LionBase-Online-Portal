Data Quality - Case¶

Problem¶

GrowBetter has created a novel greenhouse for growing rice. Currently, they have a lot of data on rice_yield, temperature, fertilizer, and water. They also used to keep track of whether or not an individual was a largeholder farmer or a smallholder farmer, but stopped because they did not find it useful.

A year later, the government is now introducing rewards for working with smallholder farmers, who have historically been hit very hard by climate change. GrowBetter would now like to find a way to classify the farmers they currently serve and have provided you an old dataset of when they used to keep track of farmer data to do this.

They already have an an analyst on their team, however this analyst is split across many data projects right now and would like you to prepare the data for analysis.
